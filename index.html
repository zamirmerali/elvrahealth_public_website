How It Works

From Fax to Decision
in Seconds

Elvra plugs into your existing fax and document workflow. No new hardware. No EHR migration. Just clarity.

  1. 1

    Instant Referral Extraction

    Elvra reads every page of an incoming referral, pulling patient demographics, diagnosis, referring physician, and clinical history.

  2. 2

    AI-Generated Summaries

    Physicians and admins receive a concise, structured summary of every referral. Understand the full clinical picture in under 30 seconds. No more reading 28-page packages.

  3. 3

    Urgency Triage

    Elvra applies AI trained on hundreds of specialty referrals to categorize each referral so your booking team always knows what to prioritize and why.

  4. 4

    Missing Investigation Alerts

    Incomplete referrals delay care. Elvra flags missing bloodwork, imaging, or consult notes and prompts your admin team to follow up before the appointment is booked.
